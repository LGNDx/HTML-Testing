The Batman

About Batman

Batman is a hero who resides on the outskirts of Gotham City. After witnessing the murder of his parents, he became who he was and stared to seek justice against criminals.

-The Batman in action.

Facts about The Batman

  1. Batman first appeared in comics on March 30th, 1939.
  2. Batman's secret identity is Bruce Wayne, a wealthy American playboy, philanthropist, and owner of Wayne Enterprises.
  3. Batman does not possess any inhuman superpowers. He does, however, possess a genius-level intellect and is a peerless martial artist, and his vast wealth affords him an extraordinary arsenal of weaponry and equipment.
  4. A large assortment of villains make up Batman's rogues gallery, including his nemesis, the Joker.
